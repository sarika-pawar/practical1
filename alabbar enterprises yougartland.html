YOGURTLAND

In 2006, Yogurtland opened its first location, offering the industry's best quality frozen yogurt made from real Californian milk served with a wide array of premium, popular toppings. Today, Yogurtland leads the industry with the finest real frozen yogurt that is smooth and creamy with superior toppings. Customers can create and control their own flavor experience in a clean and bright environment.

The Yogurtland team has a passion for finding the perfect flavor that guests will love. Yogurtland's premium custom flavors come from real ingredients sourced from their original locations - Madagascar vanilla beans from Madagascar, Chocolate from Belgium, Maqui Berry from Patagonia. No other yogurt chain offers such original flavors and exciting combinations such New York Cheesecake, Double Cookies and Cream, Peanut Butter, Pistachio, Strawberry Lemonade Sorbet, Blueberry Tart, Fresh Strawberry, French Vanilla, Pecan Praline, Dutch Chocolate, Plain Tart, and Mango.Combining these flavors with real milk gives Yogurtland yogurt its creamy, smooth taste that 's also good for you.
